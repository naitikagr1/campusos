# CampusOS 🎓

CampusOS is a unified operating system designed to simplify and modernize campus life by bringing students, faculty, administration, academics, events, and communication into a single platform.

## Problem

Colleges often rely on multiple disconnected systems for academics, attendance, events, notices, communication, and student resources. This creates inefficiencies and a fragmented experience for students and administrators.

## Solution

CampusOS provides a centralized platform where students, faculty, and administrators can manage everything related to campus life from a single dashboard.

## Key Features

### Student Portal
- Academic dashboard
- Attendance tracking
- Assignment management
- Timetable access
- Exam schedules

### Campus Community
- Student feed
- Clubs & societies
- Event management
- Announcements
- Campus discussions

### Faculty Tools
- Attendance management
- Assignment distribution
- Student communication
- Performance tracking

### Administration
- Campus analytics
- Notice management
- Resource management
- Student records
